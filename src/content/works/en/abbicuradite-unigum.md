With Kipo Produzioni we built the website for Unigum's "Abbi Cura di Te" project — a leading company in workplace safety. The project aims to raise awareness around safety through an important message: protecting yourself means taking care of yourself. Unigum collaborated with painter Rindori, who created variations of famous artworks where the protagonists wear the PPE (personal protective equipment) sold by the company. The gallery includes style proposals, mockups, early drafts, and screenshots of the final site.
