We built the new car listings website for the "M&L Auto" showroom in Montevarchi (AR), a well-known Suzuki and Hyundai dealer in the area. Their previous site was practically abandoned: slow, often failing to load content properly, freezing, and the listings were completely useless because they were not indexed and were hard to browse. After two initial design proposals we faithfully recreated the version chosen by the client, delivering an adaptable, SEO-oriented site in little time, with automations for entering car data into listings to simplify Luca's workflow.
