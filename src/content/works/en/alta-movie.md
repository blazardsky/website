Giovanni is a Kipo collaborator, with whom we have produced videos and footage for the agency's client advertising. In 2022 he asked for help building the website for his aerial filming agency — one he could manage on his own. I helped him create a custom WordPress site with bespoke code customizations and a page builder so he could modify and expand the site independently. The design has changed a bit since the beginning, but it has remained coherent and functional. The illustrations on the homepage were created by one of Giovanni's collaborators; photos and videos are naturally self-produced.
