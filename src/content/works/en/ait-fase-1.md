The Accademia Italiana del Tartufo nel mondo (AIT) was founded in 2014 from an idea by professionals from various fields. My dad is its vice president. They urgently needed a website for their association, so we decided to structure the project in two phases. The first phase was to create a site with only the essential pages and content, without neglecting user experience and a UI aligned with their "Stile Tartufo" magazine. To build this first site I used Astro.build and Tailwind, which allowed me to deliver a fast, responsive website in very little time. The second phase involves migrating the project into those managed by Kìpo srl, so they can have a dedicated team not only for website development but also for ads, SEO, social media, and more. In this second phase we will add new content and give academy members independence in creating posts and managing the site through a CMS with various integrations.
