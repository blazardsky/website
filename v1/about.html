About Me:

Aspirant shoemaker, artisan, designer and illustrator.

I am a former student of philosophy at UNIFI (dropped out at the end of the 2nd year), currently studying illustration at Nemo Academy. My interests range from graphic and web design to web and software development. I am also actively interested in arts, philosophy, and crafts.

At the moment I am cooperating with Feed Group1,
for whom I have developed websites such as Feed Group's own web page and Tenuta LeMuricce2.
As a freelancer I have also built Seth Burton's Official Website3, Sophia Consulenze3 and Mad Dj's Official Website1.

In addition to web developement I enjoy creating illustrations, logos and marks, fonts, icons as well as photo and video editing, 3d sculpting/modelling...
For a career, I would like to illustrate beer and other beverages' packagings and/or playing cards and tabletop games.
